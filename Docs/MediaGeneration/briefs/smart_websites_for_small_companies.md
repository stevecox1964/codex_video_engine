# Smart Websites For Small Companies

## Goal

Create a short practical promo video explaining why small companies need smart websites. The message should feel useful to local business owners and small teams: a modern website is not just a digital brochure, it should answer questions, capture leads, book appointments, and build trust while the owner is busy doing the work.

## Audience

Small company owners, solo operators, consultants, local service businesses, repair shops, trades, and small teams that rely on customer inquiries.

## Format

- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- Target duration: 20 seconds
- Style: clean cinematic business-tech promo, realistic, modern, practical
- Voiceover/script: yes
- Required text: simple short overlays added in the edit layer
- References: no external references yet

## Constraints

- Budget: start with one storyboard still per scene before generating video clips
- Must include: outdated website problem, lead capture, booking/contact, trust, small-company owner benefit
- Must avoid: unreadable generated UI text, fake logos, cluttered dashboards, overly corporate stock-video feel

## Approval Points

- Approve scene plan before paid generation.
- Approve still frames before image-to-video.
- Approve final clips before assembly.
